Our History

Born in Yıldız: Established within Yıldız Technical University, our Orienteering Team has been introducing YTÜ youth to the sport of orienteering for years, working together with experienced students and alumni for their development. Students develop their leadership and strategy competencies alongside their sports skills.

Growing in Competitions: Established in 2011, our team has consistently shown its presence in the field and has been a team that elevates its performance every season. In the orienteering branch, which combines physical endurance with mental discipline, we regularly take the start in different age and category groups, providing active representation in official federation organizations and inter-university races.

Recent Successes

In the 2024-2025 season alone, the team has achieved 51 Medals and 7 Trophies. The athletes raised by the Yıldız Technical Orienteering team have made our university proud and become a source of inspiration for many young talents with their performances in national and inter-university organizations.

Professional Team Mission

Orienteering is not just a sport, it is a discipline that teaches thinking, patience, and making the right decisions in difficult moments. In our team, athletes do not only learn to use a compass and a map, they learn to make decisions amid uncertainty, to see mistakes as a natural part of the process, and to remain calm under pressure. Our athletes do not just aim to reach the finish line, they learn to fall, to rise, and not just to succeed, but to succeed rightfully.

Our mission is to position orienteering not merely as a racing sport, but as a discipline that represents analytical thinking, nature awareness, and sports ethics within a strong culture of family and friendship.
