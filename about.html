About Our Club

Established in 2011, we have consistently shown our presence on the field[cite: 128].

Our History [cite: 117]

Born at Yıldız Technical University[cite: 118]. We have been introducing YTU youth to orienteering for years, working together with experienced students and alumni[cite: 119]. Our students develop leadership and strategic skills alongside their athletic abilities[cite: 120].

Our Mission [cite: 174]

To position orienteering not just as a racing sport, but as a discipline representing analytical thinking, nature awareness, and sports ethics within a strong family and friendship culture[cite: 172]. Our ultimate goal is to make this sport more visible at our university and represent YTU in national and international tracks[cite: 173].

Team Values [cite: 178]

  • Leadership: Developing leadership spirit on the field and in life[cite: 179, 180].
  • Team Spirit: Emphasizing collective power rather than individual success[cite: 181, 182].
  • Hard Work: Sweating to be better every day[cite: 183, 184].
  • Ethics and Discipline: Teaching not just to win, but to win righteously[cite: 185, 186].
  • National and Social Awareness: Raising individuals beneficial to family, nation, and humanity[cite: 187, 188].
  • Justice and Respect: Respect for the opponent, rules, and effort[cite: 189, 190].
  • Nature Awareness: Doing sports with environmental responsibility and protecting nature[cite: 191, 192].
